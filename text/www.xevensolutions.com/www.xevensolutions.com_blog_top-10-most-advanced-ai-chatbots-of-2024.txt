Artificial intelligence (AI) chatbots are becoming increasingly sophisticated and used in various industries, including customer service, healthcare, and education. AI chatbots can provide several benefits, such as reducing costs, improving customer satisfaction, and freeing human employees to focus on more complex tasks. This article includes the list of 10 Top AI chatbots for 2024. Also, perform the comparative analysis on what chatbot is best in which scenario.
Best AI Chatbots of 2024
Here is a list of the top 10 AI chatbots of 2024:
1: Google Bard
Google Bard is another new AI chatbot that is still under development. However, it has already shown great promise and can carry on natural and engaging conversations with users. Bard can also access and process information from the real world through Google Search, making it a powerful tool for learning and research.

2: ChatGPT
ChatGPT is a large language model chatbot developed by OpenAI. It is one of the most advanced AI chatbots available. It can be used for various tasks, such as generating text, translating languages, and writing creative content. ChatGPT can also learn from user interactions and adapt its responses accordingly.
3: Microsoft Bing Chat
Microsoft Bing Chat is a new AI chatbot that is currently in beta testing. It is based on the same technology as ChatGPT and can perform many of the same tasks. However, Bing Chat is also integrated with the Microsoft Bing search engine, which allows it to provide more comprehensive and informative answers to user queries.
4: Legal Contract Analyzer
AI-powered legal contract analyzer software can help you understand and manage your agreements more easily. It helps speed up how long it takes to review paperwork between companies. It also helps negotiations go better and companies reduce risks. This software lets you quickly review contracts, deals, and legal documents. It evaluates everything to help make sure everything is on the up and up and follows the rules. Overall, it aims to simplify what can otherwise be a tedious process.

5: AI – Recruitment App
AI Recruitment App makes hiring easier by collecting data to find the best job matches. It saves time for both employers and job seekers by streamlining the whole process. The app tracks emails conveniently and keeps all information secure online.
6: LawGPT
Advanced AI-driven LawGPT solution is tailored to the complex world of legal practice.. This platform empowers legal professionals by offering comprehensive tools designed to streamline and enhance their legal work. LawGPT leverages artificial intelligence to simplify legal tasks, making law practice more efficient and effective, from document analysis and drafting to contract review and research. It aims to bridge the gap between traditional legal practices and cutting-edge technology.
7: Jasper Chat
Jasper Chat is an AI chatbot designed for marketing and sales teams. It can be used to generate personalized marketing messages, create sales scripts, and even qualify leads. Jasper Chat can also integrate with various marketing and sales software applications, such as email marketing platforms and CRM systems.
8: Chat by Copy.ai
Chat by Copy.ai is an AI chatbot that is designed to help businesses generate creative content, such as blog posts, social media posts, and email newsletters. It can also be used to generate product descriptions, sales copy, and other types of marketing materials. Chat by Copy.ai is a powerful tool for businesses that want to produce high-quality content without having to hire a team of writers.
9: Chatsonic
Chatsonic is an AI chatbot that is designed for individuals in the creative industries. It can be used to generate ideas, write different types of creative content, and even edit and proofread existing content. Chatsonic is a powerful tool for creative professionals who want to improve their productivity and creativity.
10: Babylon Healthcare AI Chatbot
Babylon AI chatbot is an artificial intelligence-powered healthcare AI Chatbot developed by Babylon Health. It uses natural language processing to understand user queries and generate responses through text conversations. It aims to provide basic healthcare information to users through conversational interactions in a simple question-answer format. Users can describe their symptoms and medical history to get guidance.

Benefits of AI Chatbots
AI chatbots offer several benefits, including:
Reduced costs: AI chatbots can help to reduce costs by automating tasks that human employees currently perform.
Improved customer satisfaction: AI chatbots can help to improve customer satisfaction by providing 24/7 support, answering questions quickly and accurately, and resolving issues efficiently.
Increased sales: AI chatbots can help to increase sales by qualifying leads, generating leads, and even closing deals.
Improved employee productivity: AI chatbots can help improve employee productivity by taking on routine tasks, freeing employees to focus on more complex and value-added activities.
Enhanced data collection: AI chatbots can collect valuable data about customer interactions, which can be used to improve products and services, develop targeted marketing campaigns, and make better business decisions.
