In the age of artificial intelligence, generative AI video has evolved significantly. 
Instead of simply combining clips from a library, you can now transform a text prompt into a lifelike video using AI video generators. 
These tools are so powerful that they can generate high-quality, visually engaging videos in minutes, saving time and effort. 
As new AI video generator tools hit the market every other day, we have compiled a list of the top 5 best AI video generators to help narrow your search.
5 Best AI Video Generators in 2025

1: Luma Labs Dream Machine
Pricing: Start at $30 per month
Want to create an AI video in which every shot feels realistic? Luma Labs Dream Machine is the best answer. 
It is an AI video generator tool that can create professional-looking videos from a text prompt or image in just a few minutes. 
It can improve the descriptive idea that the user gives it and create a high-quality video that includes multiple shots. 
You can create a 5 seconds long video that can be extended, but it may make some unexpected changes to your original video. 
Luma Lab Dream Machine captures motion concepts more effectively than other AI video generator tools. 
The free plan comes with 30 video creations, while you can create 120 videos with its paid plan. 
Pros:

Great video quality


5-second long video and clip extension


Text and image prompt to video


Impressive motion

Cons:

Slower processing time


Less additional features
Expensive compared to other tools

2: Synthesia
Pricing: Starts at $18/month
If you are a marketer, Synthesia is the best video AI generator that saves time and helps you make videos for your service or product. 
It turns your text prompt into a lifelike video using an AI avatar. 
Synthesia comes with more than 160 avatars with micro gestures and lets the users choose their native language from 140 language options. 
The best part? Users can create their own avatars and even clone their voices, adding a personalized touch to the video. 
Pros

160 multilingual AI Avatars 
Ability to create custom AI avatar
Best for product demos, training videos, and explainer videos
35+ integrations with other platforms
Automatic translation and 60+ video templates

Cons

Best for small screens
Limited options for expressions and appearance 

Curious about how to use AI to scale up the content creation process? Here’s our breakdown of the best AI sentence generators. 
3: Runway Gen-3 Alpha
Pricing: starts at $15 per month
As marketed, the frontier of video generation, Runway Gen-3 Alpha is a powerful AI video generation tool that offers advanced motion control, multi-motion brush, and 10-second long video. 
With text-to-video, it also supports image-to-video outputs. 
The highlights include a multi-motion brush for selecting areas to generate more precise expressions and creating human-like characters with gestures and expressions. 
This advanced AI video generation tool offers a free plan with 125 credits but with no upscale or watermark removal option. The standards plan starts at $15 per month for 625 credits.
Pros

Multi-motion brush to animate desired expressions and gestures
Fine grain control over motion
Lip-syncing 
Asset library and timeline editor

Cons

The final video might look a little shaky
Expensive compared to other tools

4: Colossyan
Pricing: Starts at $19/month
Another of the best AI video generator apps on our list is Colossyan, which supports video creation from text prompts. 
Whether you want to create tutorials or marketing content for social media platforms, Colossyan is your go-to tool where you can add multilingual AI avatars with natural expressions for more dynamic presentation. 
It will not only help you streamline your content creation process but also work great for AI video editing. 
Users can customize the appearance, emotions, and age of AI avatars to cater to their specific needs and preferences. 
They can even add multiple AI actors to a scene to make their video more engaging. 
In addition, it offers automated translations in multiple languages and enables users to resize videos in different aspect ratios.
Pros

Multilingual Capabilities (70+ languages)
Translations in 26 languages
Fast generation time
Ability to add multiple AI actors in one scene

Cons:

AI avatar limitation
Avatar lacks emotional depth

5: Haiper AI

Pricing: Starts at $8/month
Haiper AI helps you create videos from text and images in just a few clicks. The platform is user-friendly and supports the customization of a generated video. 
It also allows users to include and edit their own videos. Haiper AI offers impressive prompt adherence, and its AI model can predict accurate motion. 
Its free plan offers up to 10 creations per day for free, but these videos contain a watermark, which means users can’t use them for commercial purposes. However, its paid plans come with advanced features that cater to different levels of use.
Pros

Text-to-speech feature
Ability to predict accurate motion
Simple interface and is easy to use
Good prompt adherence

Cons

Short video clips
No watermark-free videos for free plan
Limited Language Support

Conclusion:
With AI video generator tools, we see how far artificial intelligence can go when it comes to understanding human-like avatars and motion. 
Now, anyone can create cinematic videos or use realistic AI avatars to create product demos without spending big bucks.
These tools give you new ways to be more innovative and tell stories in a better way. Whether you are a marketer or a creator, use these tools to improve the workflow of your video projects and save time.
