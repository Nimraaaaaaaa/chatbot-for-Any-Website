In today’s article, we will discuss the top 10 AI text generator tools to use in 2024 and their pros and cons. But before that, let’s briefly introduce AI text generators.
What are AI text generators?

AI text generators are software applications that generate text based on the input provided. They utilize advanced technologies such as natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLM), and machine learning algorithms to generate human-like content quickly. 
It is necessary to keep in mind that AI text generator tools are to assist writers rather than to replace them. These tools augment professionals’ abilities in crafting high-quality content more efficiently and creatively.
Top 10 AI Text Generator Tools for 2024
Tools have helped several professionals from different domains in their day-to-day tasks. Frequently Asked Questions
How do AI text generator tools work?
AI text generator tools work on a transformer architecture. It does not rely on recurrence and convolutions to generate an output. The algorithm is trained on large datasets from multiple sources, including the Internet and other reliable sources. Machine learning and deep learning models utilize these datasets to extract, trigger, and decide on relevant output.
Is there a tool to detect AI generated text?
Several AI tools are available to detect AI-generated text, including Kazan SEO, Content at Scale, Undetectable.ai, etc. However, these tools are not 100% accurate and misidentify human-generated content as AI-generated text.
