After the launch of Chat GPT, AI sentence generators have gained popularity and made writer’s block a thing of the past. 
These AI text-generation tools not only help you kick-start your writing process but also create original content based on your prompts. 
As premium and free AI text generators launch almost daily, choosing one that works efficiently and produces engaging content has become difficult.
To help you, we have rounded up the 5 top AI sentence generators with their features, pros and cons, and pricing. 
Are you ready? Let’s get into it!
What are AI sentence generators?
AI sentence generators are AI-powered tools that use models trained on text data to understand and produce sentences based on user input.   
Best AI sentence generators in 2025
When choosing the AI sentence generator, you must consider what you want to accomplish with this tool. 
Knowing your goals will significantly help you find the right one, as not all AI writing tools perform equally well for different writing tasks. For instance, some models excel at social media copywriting, while others are better at drafting blog posts. 
Here are our list of best AI sentence generators that work best for advanced writing tasks: 
Best AI sentence Generators
Best AI sentence Generators
1-Jasper
Pricing: Creator monthly plan for $49/month 
When it comes to AI text generation tools, Jasper tops the charts due to its ability to create content of any type and length. 
In addition to creating high-quality content, you can also customize the content to align with your style guide and brand voice. 
It comes with over 50 templates and outperforms other tools for writing social media captions, marketing copy, and video scripts. Its text output is powerful and accurate. 
Above all, it can be integrated with other tools, such as Zapier and Surfer SEO. 
Pros 

Text output sounds well-written and accurate and passes the plagiarism checker
It allows you to integrate with Surfer SEO to optimize your blog posts
It has over 50 AI marketing copy templates and a rewrite feature
Jasper offers unlimited AI-generated words for creator plans, which can be an excellent option for small businesses.

Cons

Jasper struggles to create highly technical and niche topics
It is pricey compared to other AI sentence-generator tools

2-Quillbot
Pricing: $19.95 per month
Quilltbot Flow is among the top AI text generators specifically designed for academic writing. The QuillBot Flow has plenty of features that save you time and act as your writing assistance from your first to final draft. 
This free AI text generator is also perfect for crafting content for product descriptions, long-form articles, email copy, and social media captions. 
In addition to content creation, it comes with many other features such as paraphrasing, citation generation, grammar checking, AI detection, and text summarization. 
The best part? All these features are free to use. 
Pros

It is a pretty affordable AI text generator as a monthly plan costs $19.95
AI tools are completely free 
Quillbot is available as a browser extension for Google Chrome and macOS, 

Cons

Free and paid plans come with character limitations
Users can access only two writing modes for free

3– Koala Writer AI
Pricing: Starts from $9 per month 
Another powerful tool is Koala Writer, which works best for writing SEO-friendly content. 
One of the key features of Koala Writer that sets it apart from other AI text generators is that it uses data from the content that appears on Google’s first page. 
When you provide a blog outline and the main keyword you want to rank for, it helps you create content optimized to rank better on Google for that keyword.
It also provides an editing feature and helps you make adjustments if you don’t like the desired output.
Pros:

It analyzes Google’s top-ranking results to create relevant content
It runs SERP analysis and optimize your blogs
It offers extensive language support

Cons:

Its free plan has a character limitation
It may not understand your brand voice of tone

4- Writesonic

Pricing: $20 per month for individual
Writesonic is one of the best AI for generating text tools for writing high-quality marketing copy at a low price.
 In addition to producing social media ads and LinkedIn descriptions, it also helps you to rephrase content for a more polished output. 
It supports more than 25 languages to create content, making it a great text-generation tool for users worldwide. 
Writesonic can be integrated with SurfurSEO to optimize your content for search engines and bring keyword data into your writing process.  
Pros

It has a simple interface, making it easy for beginners
With content creation, it can also be helpful for keyword search
It provides fast and accurate responses

Cons

It requires more than one keyword to write long-form content
Its image-generation tool does not work properly and provides inconsistent results

5-Grammarly
Pricing: $25 per month for businesses
Grammarly is a renowned AI writing assistance and grammar checker tool. Its AI writing assistant feature works across text editors like MS Word and Google Docs and email services like Outlook and Gmail. 
It can be integrated with email services, helping you compose and reply to emails by generating responses when you hit the reply button.
 It comes with plenty of free templates. Whether you want to write job descriptions and articles, you can use its free templates to produce content. 
Above all, it can help you create high-quality content when paired with other AI sentence generators.
Pros

It is an affordable AI sentence generator, and its top-tier plan allows you to generate text with 2,000 AI prompts.
With composing emails, you can generate email replies with just one click.
It also helps you rewrite sentences, adjust your writing tone, and develop a custom content style guide.

Cons

It is not good for producing long-form content
It may not help to generate content ideas
