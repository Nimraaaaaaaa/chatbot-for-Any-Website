What Is MrPos? 



 



							MrPos is a restaurant management system that provides cloud-based solutions to cover all the needs in a single package. This system is designed to perform all the tasks accurately and easily automate the business to operate the activities. MrPos software helps restaurants to focus on their customer service experiences. This versatile point of sale (POS) software is available in different modes like web-server-based and mobile applications.
